Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do I get leads?
Leads are delivered instantly via text and email. Most of our roofers call within 5 minutes of receiving a lead.
What information do I get with each lead?
You get the homeowner's name, phone, email, ZIP code, plus our AI analysis of their roof's estimated age and condition.
Can I pause or cancel anytime?
Yes! There are no contracts. You can pause lead delivery anytime from your dashboard, and you only pay for leads you receive.
What if I get a bad lead?
We have a lead quality guarantee. If you get a lead with a wrong number or fake info, we'll replace it or credit your account.
How do you get these leads?
Homeowners find us through Google, Facebook, and word of mouth. They use our free AI roof analysis tool, and if their roof needs work, they can request quotes from local roofers.
